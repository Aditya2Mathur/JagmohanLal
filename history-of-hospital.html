History and Hospital Profile

Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital

A High-Volume Cataract & Training Centre for Aspiring Ophthalmologists

Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh

Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital is a rapidly growing, surgeon-focused eye care institution offering exceptional clinical exposure, hands-on surgical training, and modern infrastructure. Since its inception in 2016, the hospital has evolved from a modest rented facility into a state-of-the-art, purpose-built eye hospital, dedicated to excellence in cataract surgery and ophthalmic education.

Our Journey

Build Surgical Confidence. Train in a High-Volume Eye Hospital.

Established in 2016, Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital has grown from a rented facility into a fully owned, modern eye care center located at Awas Vikas Colony near Bareilly Mod, Shahjahanpur. It has earned a strong reputation for excellence in cataract surgery and community eye care services.

The hospital provides an ideal learning and training environment for young ophthalmologists seeking hands-on surgical exposure and structured clinical growth.

Infrastructure Platform

Equipped with precision technology designed specifically to enhance training efficiency, our systematic OT and OPD workflow is built to scale learning.
