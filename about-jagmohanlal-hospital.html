About JagmohanLal Hospital

A High-Volume Cataract & Training Centre for Aspiring Ophthalmologists

Why Train at Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital?

Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital is a rapidly growing, surgeon-focused eye care institution offering exceptional clinical exposure, hands-on surgical training, and modern infrastructure. Since its inception in 2016, the hospital has evolved from a modest rented facility into a state-of-the-art, purpose-built eye hospital, dedicated to excellence in cataract surgery and ophthalmic education.

Our Journey

  • Established: 2016
  • Initial Location: Rented building, Shahjahanpur
  • Current Facility: Own hospital building at Awas Vikas Colony, near Bareilly Mod, Shahjahanpur

This transformation reflects our commitment to long-term growth, quality eye care, and structured ophthalmic training.

World-Class Infrastructure

Designed specifically for high-volume ophthalmology services and training:

  • 4 Modular Operation Theatres
  • Multiple Consultation Rooms
  • Dedicated Refraction Room
  • Vision Gallery for detailed vision assessment
  • Pre-op, Post-op & Recovery areas
