Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hospital for eye treatment in Shahjahanpur?

Jagmohan Lal Hospital is one of the most trusted eye hospitals in Shahjahanpur. We offer cataract, LASIK, glaucoma, and other advanced eye treatments under one roof.

How much does cataract surgery cost in Shahjahanpur?

Cataract surgery cost depends on the lens type and eye condition. At our hospital, surgery starts at affordable rates with safe procedures and fast recovery.

Can LASIK remove glasses permanently?

Yes, LASIK laser treatment helps remove or reduce the need for glasses. It is safe, quick, and gives long-lasting clear vision.

How often should I get my eyes checked?

Adults should get an eye check-up once every year. Diabetic or elderly people may need more frequent check-ups.

Is eye surgery painful?

No, most eye surgeries like cataract or LASIK are painless. Doctors use local anaesthesia, and patients can go home the same day.

Do you treat children’s eye problems?

Yes, we have a Pediatric Ophthalmology department for children. We treat lazy eye, squint, vision errors, and other eye issues in kids.

How can I book an appointment at Jagmohan Lal Hospital?

You can call our hospital, visit directly, or book an appointment online through our website or WhatsApp for faster confirmation.
