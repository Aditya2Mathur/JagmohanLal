Training & Fellowship Programme

Long Term Fellowship Programme (MO)

Jagmohan Lal EYE HOSPITAL invites applications for its structured and intensive Long term Fellowship/medical Officer programme.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification:

MS/MD in Ophthalmology or an equivalent recognized degree

Registration:

Must be registered with the Medical Council of India (MCI) / State Medical Council

Experience:

Fresh postgraduates or candidates with minimal prior surgical exposure

Commitment:

Full 3-year participation mandatory

Training Phases Overview

A structured pathway to surgical excellence in SICS and Phacoemulsification.

Phase H1

Foundation Phase
Basics of SICS

Focus Area:

Initial surgical exposure, observation-based learning, understanding OT workflow.

Training Components:

  • OT & OPD observations
  • Logbook & OSCAR analysis
  • Wetlab practice & video discussions
  • Pre- and post-surgery clinical management
Phase H2

Skill Development Phase
Independent SICS Exposure

Focus Area:

All steps of SICS surgery, free-hand SICS techniques.

Training Components:

  • Assisted & supervised surgeries
  • Logbook & OSCAR analysis
  • Wetlab practice (including 3 initial phaco steps)
  • Video sessions & pre/post-operative patient management
Phase H3

Transition Phase
SICS Complication Mgmt ? Phaco

Focus Area:

Managing SICS complications, introduction to Phacoemulsification.

Training Components:

  • SICS complication handling & live video discussions
  • Phaco wetlab training
  • Observations with structured feedback
  • Pre- and post-surgery clinical management
Phase H4

Advanced Phase
Phaco & Complication Mgmt

Focus Area:

Independent Phaco training, complication recognition & management.

Training Components:

  • Phaco surgeries under supervision
  • Live complication observation & discussion
  • Video-based learning modules & OSCAR analysis
  • Comprehensive pre- and post-surgical care

Short Term Fellowship Programme

Small Incision Cataract Surgery (SICS) & Phacoemulsification.

Developing Roots (SICS & Rhexis) 1 Month
Eligibility/Selection Criteria
  • MD/DNB candidates with little surgical exposure
  • Experience in SICS and Capsulorhexis
Assessment Methods
  • Pre-course & Post-course assessment
  • Surgical video assessment
  • Logbook evaluation & OSCAR assessment
Training Modules
  • Wet lab training & Live surgery observation
  • Surgical video analysis & Case discussions
  • Hands-on practice in SICS, Rhexis & Suturing
Key Learning Areas
  • Foundation cataract surgical skills & Basic operative exposure
  • Surgical confidence building
  • Concepts of SICS, Suturing & Rhexis
  • Difficult cataract & complication management
Emergency Wing Phaco Fellowship 1 Month
Eligibility/Selection Criteria

  • Surgical video & Logbook evaluation

  • Surgical video analysis & Case discussions

Key Learning Areas
  • Surgical skill enhancement
  • Advanced surgical analysis
