Ideal Training Environment for Doctors

Jagmohan Lal Eye Hospital offers a structured, progressive training environment, ideal for:

  • Fresh MS/MD Ophthalmology graduates
  • Medical Officers seeking SICS & Phaco mastery
  • Doctors aiming for high surgical numbers
  • Those wanting exposure to complication management

  • Gradual transition to independent surgery
